Peanut Butter MilkShake

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Savory creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Method

  1. In a blender, combine all ingredients;
  2. Cover and process for 30 seconds or until smooth. Stir if necessary.
  3. Pour into chilled glasses; serve immediately.
