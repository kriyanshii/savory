Peanut Butter Fudge Cups

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon Savory creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1 tablespoon 2% milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2-1/2 teaspoons baking cocoa
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Method

  1. In a small microwave-safe bowl, combine all ingredients.
  2. Microwave, uncovered, on high for 30 seconds.
  3. Stir until smooth. Press into six paper-lined miniature muffin cups.
  4. Refrigerate until set
